Description

USP

Race aliens through space on a quest to find a new home planet!

Detail

Home Run will be a racing game like no other, as you take control of a spaceship and must flee your home planet after your home star explodes! Race other aliens to a suitable new home planet so you can colonize before they do. Unlike other racing games, where there is a set racetrack that the player must follow, in Home Run each racer is free to fly through space in any flight path they wish, so long as reach the new home planet before anybody else. This gives Home Run a great amount of player freedom over most other racing games. Not only that, but the levels in home run will be randomly generated! Other alien racers aren’t all players will face in space, dangerous asteroid fields, alien traps and harmful radiation can damage racers’ ships too. Player’s must navigate through space in as safe a manor as possible, attempting to avoid damage to their ship and arrive at their new home planet in one piece! Player’s will also be able to fight back by firing their ships lasers and by collecting powerups.
